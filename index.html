FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need sales experience to apply?

No. About 40% of our women come from sales-adjacent roles (customer service, retail, hospitality). The other 60% are split between traditional sales reps and career pivoters with zero formal sales background. The program is built for both.

How long until I'm actually closing?

Most women land their first setter or closer role between week 4 and week 8. First real commission check typically lands in month 2-3. Average student hits a $5K month inside 90 days.

What's the time commitment?

6-10 hours a week during the cohort. Modules, live calls, and practice reps. After placement, you set your own schedule — most of our women work 25-35 hours a week once they're running a full book.

Does this work if I'm a stay-at-home mom?

Yes. A meaningful share of our community closes around childcare schedules. It takes more intention around call times, but the flexibility is one of the biggest reasons women pick this path over a corporate job.

Who is this NOT for?

Women looking for a get-rich-quick pitch or a fully passive income stream. High ticket sales is a skill. The first 60 days are hard. If you want a button to press, this isn't it.
